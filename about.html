❓ Frequently Asked Questions

Is CineZone BD legal?
Yes, CineZone BD is a movie information and review platform. We do not host or distribute copyrighted movies. We provide movie metadata from TMDB and link to official trailers on YouTube.

Do I need an account to watch trailers?
No, our platform is completely free and accessible without registration.

How often is the content updated?
Our movie database is updated in real-time using the latest trending data from international movie databases.

Can I request a movie trailer?
Yes, you can contact us via our contact form if you think a trailer is missing or needs replacement.
